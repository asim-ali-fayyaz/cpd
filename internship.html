INTERNSHIP AT CPD

Centre for Professional Development (CPD) at Air University offers internship opportunities to the students of Islamabad. Being an intern means more than getting involved in the day-to-day running of the organization. As an intern, you'll get exposure to real projects and challenges in a vibrant environment and gain real work experience. And you'll work with the brightest and best in the educational institution and participate as a member of the workforce of the Pakistan’s most recognized University.

Undergraduate internships

Our interns are sought for an assortment of functions and can benefit from learning beyond the classroom when you gain hands-on experience with the Pakistan’s most recognized University.

Graduate Internships

MBA and graduate interns are expected to develop sound personality, management and interpersonal skills, which indeed contribute to the success of an individual in his career.
