YOUTH CAREER COUNSELING

Centre for Professional Development offers career counseling to the final year students of Air University. There are a number of factors that influence your career development, including:-
Interests
Abilities
Values
Personality
Social Background
Circumstances
It is a comprehensive, developmental program designed to assist students in making occupational choices and develop student competencies in self-knowledge, educational and occupational exploration and career planning. Career counseling is more than just deciding on a job you want to get when you graduate. It is one to one session with a qualified counselor that helps individuals acquire the knowledge, skills, and experience necessary to identify options, explore alternatives and succeed in society.
Air University will not charge any fee to the students
